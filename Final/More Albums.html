Official Page
   
 

The Sound Of Madness album was Shinedowns 3rd album. Second Chance (A song on the album) hit #1 in American Rock music and stayed there for months. This song also earned 3 platinum records.

  
   
 

The Us and Them album sold over 800,000 copies internationally and hit #23 on American songs in 2005 when it came out. This album also has Gold certification.

  
   
 

Leave A Whisper is Shinedown's 1st album and was released in 2003. This album got a Platinum ranking and sold over 1,500,000 copies. This album had a song named "Burning Bright" that hit #2 on American song leaderboard in 2003.
