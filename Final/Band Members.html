Brent Smith is the lead singer of Shinedown. He was one of the band members that founded the band in 2001. He has part in every song that shinedown has made. He was born on January 10th 1978 and born in Knoxville Tennesse where he grew up. Before founding Shinedown he was part of a band called Dreve.

Zach Myers is one of the guitarists for Shinedown. Zach was born in 1983 in Memphis Tennessee. He found a back named The Fairwell. He joined Shinedown in 2005 as a temporary guitarist but officially joined the band in 2008 when the lead guitarist left.

Eric Bass is the Guitarist and Pianoist for Shinedown. He was born on October 23rd 1974 in Charleston, South Carolina. He joined Shinedown in 2007 near the same time Zach joined. Eric started playing the guitar when he was 13.
