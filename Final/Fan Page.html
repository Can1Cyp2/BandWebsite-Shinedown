Albums:

  
   
 

Threat To Survival

  
 

Threat to survival is Shinedowns most recent album. ``How Did You Love`` is Shinedowns most popular song on the album, with over 7 million views on YouTube. This song was also the #1 Rock song in america for months. Over 3 million monthly listeners on Spotify. The album came out on Sept. 18, 2015.

  
   
 

Amaryllis

  
 

Amaryllis is shinedowns 4th album. It includes big hits like "Bully" and "Enemies". The WWE used the songs "Enemies" and "Adrenaline" as theme songs to their events. The album in total sold more that 500,000 copies.
